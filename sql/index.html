第一课 我叫徐文长

我叫徐文长。 My name is Xu WenChang.
我是中国人。 I am from China.
我会讲故事。 I am good at story-telling.
我会讲花木兰的故事。 I can tell [you] stories about Hua MuLan.

第二课这是花木兰

这是花木兰。 This is Hua MuLan.
木兰是一个女孩子。 MuLan is a girl.
她今年十七岁。 She is (this year) seventeen years old..
她会织布，她会写字，她也会武术。 She can weave cloth, she can write characters, [and] she also can do martial arts.
